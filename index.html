FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What age group are the classes for?+

Classes are designed for all age groups — from children aged 6 and above to adults of any level. Harshit adapts the teaching style, pace, and examples to suit the student's age and existing knowledge.

Do I need prior chess knowledge?+

No prior knowledge is required. Complete beginners are very welcome. The Starter plan is specifically designed to take you from zero to confidently playing and understanding chess in a matter of weeks.

How do online classes work?+

Online classes are conducted live via video call using an interactive digital chessboard. Sessions are fully interactive — ask questions, analyse positions, and receive real-time feedback. Sessions can also be recorded for you to revisit later.

Where are the offline classes held?+

Offline classes are held at B-704, Pan Oasis, Sector-70, Noida, Uttar Pradesh. Please book via WhatsApp to confirm your preferred time slot before visiting.

Can I switch between online and offline?+

Yes, absolutely. You can discuss your preferred format when booking, and can switch between formats with advance notice if your circumstances change.

How do I book a class?+

Simply tap any "Book" button on this page to be redirected to WhatsApp, where you can chat directly with Harshit Singh to choose your plan, format, and schedule your first session.
